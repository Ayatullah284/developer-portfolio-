Personal life

Attaullah Khan is from Mianwali District and his hometown is Esakhel. He received his early education from Esakhel. He is traditionally considered a Saraiki which is a dialect of Punjabi language singer.[8]

Attaullah relocated to Lahore after becoming a professional musician performing in Saraiki, Urdu and English. He has been married four times and has four children. His daughter Laraib Atta is a professional VFX artist who has worked for several Hollywood films.[9][10] His son Sanwal Esakhelvi is also pursuing a career in music,[1]
